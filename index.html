About Us

Bhatheja's Egg Store (Since: 1985)

Bhatheja's Egg Store is one of the oldest egg vendors in Shahabad Markanda situated in District of Kurukshetra also Known as Dharma Bhoomi. Store is Presently Run By Mr. Dharmaveer Who took over the business after his father who established it in early 1985. Store's Trust and Reputation in Dealing of wide variety of eggs is unquestionable since day one and continues till date. Bhatheja's Deal in Retail , Mass Supply and Take Order within and outside town also with services like E-Orders, Home Delivery and Online Payment.
